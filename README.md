# 🛒 Blinkit Sales & Performance Analysis Dashboard

A dynamic and interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyze Blinkit's grocery sales data, focusing on sales performance, outlet efficiency, product categories, and customer preferences. The report provides actionable insights through KPI-driven visualizations and interactive analysis.

---

The **Blinkit Sales & Performance Analysis Dashboard** is a multi-page Power BI report designed to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights. The dashboard helps stakeholders monitor key performance indicators, analyze outlet and product performance, and support data-driven decision-making.

---

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

* 📊 **Power BI Desktop** – Main data visualization platform used for report development.
* 📂 **Power Query** – Used for data cleaning, transformation, and preparation.
* 🧠 **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Used for calculated measures, KPIs, and dynamic analysis.
* 🔗 **Data Modeling** – Relationships established to enable filtering and aggregation.
* 📁 **File Format** – `.pbix` for development and `.png` for dashboard previews.

---


The dataset contains information related to:

* Product Categories
* Item Fat Content
* Sales Amount
* Product Ratings
* Outlet Type
* Outlet Size
* Outlet Location Type
* Outlet Establishment Year
* Item Visibility

The dataset is structured and was cleaned and transformed using Power Query before visualization.

---

## • Business Problem

Quick-commerce businesses generate massive volumes of sales data. However, raw transactional data alone does not provide clear visibility into:

* Which outlet types contribute the most revenue.
* Which product categories drive sales growth.
* How customer preferences vary across products.
* Which areas require operational improvements.

Without proper analysis, identifying trends and making informed decisions becomes difficult.

---

## • Goal of the Dashboard

The objective of this dashboard is to provide an interactive business intelligence solution that enables stakeholders to:

* Monitor overall sales performance.
* Analyze outlet-wise performance.
* Understand product category trends.
* Track KPIs efficiently.
* Support data-driven decision-making.

## • Business Impact & Insights

### 📌 Key Insights

* Tier 3 outlets generated the highest sales contribution.
* Fruits & Vegetables and Snack Foods emerged as top-performing categories.
* Low-fat products accounted for approximately 65% of overall sales.
* Sales patterns varied significantly across outlet types and locations.
* Product visibility influenced category performance.
* KPI tracking enabled quick identification of high-performing and low-performing areas.

### 📈 Business Impact

* Enables faster business decision-making through interactive reporting.
* Helps identify high-performing outlets and product categories.
* Supports performance monitoring using KPI-driven dashboards.
* Improves visibility into sales trends and customer preferences.
* Facilitates strategic planning and operational optimization.
